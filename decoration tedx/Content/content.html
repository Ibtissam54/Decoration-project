Easy deco tips with big results

6 ways with wall décor

Getting a cosy living feeling at home doesn’t have to be expensive or challenging. Here are six simple tips on how to lift your interior using wall décor.

1. Create your own mood board

A noticeboard provides flexibility, so you can change your deco to suit the season or whatever’s inspiring you right now. Draw attention to your wall when night falls with atmospheric lighting such as the timeless SKURUP wall lamp for an industrial touch.

2. Mix and match photo frames

Dare to blend various styles of frames for your own unique look that’s playful too. Extend your art wall by using surfaces such as desks or drawers against the wall to display more items, making your gallery wall the room’s focal point.

3. Bring dead spaces to life

Find a plain wall in your home – corners next to windows are a great choice as they benefit from natural light. Hang rows of MOSSLANDA picture ledges on the wall, then add your favourite framed prints. Pair with neutral-coloured curtains to let your art wall do the talking.

4. Design some DIY art

You don’t have to be a skilled artist to paint your own art. Start by choosing a colour you like and play about painting different abstract shapes. Tape your finished masterpiece to the wall using washi tape so you can reposition and change your artwork without damaging your walls.

5. Make a modern display with storage

Kill two birds with one stone by using a smart pegboard to organise your home and decorate your wall at the same time. Add various accessories such as hooks, shelves and containers that are just as easy to remove and replace as they are to attach for maximum flexibility.

6. Save time with ready to hang art

Start by choosing a set of prints that illustrates something dear to you, here it’s travel. Arrange the pictures to create your own wall collage and display your knick-knacks and memorabilia that fit the theme in a classic glass cabinet such as this MILSBO one underneath.

Style your home with indoor plant decor

Surround yourself with decorative plants and watch yourself bloom. Aside from making homes look pleasant, science says they boost well-being, reduce stress and help with creativity. Here are a couple of easy-to-do decoration tips – straight from our plant-loving interior designers and creatives – that will make your home a lush oasis in no time.

1. Group plants together to create an urban
jungle

Create a lush vibe by placing a mix of plants on plant stands.
A good rule of thumb for a visually pleasing arrangement:
place larger, leafier plants on the bottom shelves or on the
floor, and smaller ones on the upper shelves.

2. A truly evergreen wall

Create a lush vertical garden with – wait for it – artificial plants. Before you shake your head, think of it: they’re super easy to put together with a simple click, and can be used indoors or outdoors. Best of all, they don’t require a green thumb – no watering needed.

3. Minimal plant pots for maximum impact

CGoing for a sleek and minimal look, but still want plenty of plants? Then choose plant pots in the same colour – but in different sizes to fit your plants – to keep the look clean and elegantly tied together.

4. Flowers that are always fresh

Place tall-stemmed flowers and branches – that never wilt and never need water – in clear glass vases for a romantic look. Clear objects let light through and create interesting silhouettes when placed next to a window.

5. An ode to nature

Create an inspiring still-life setting with various plants and flowers in different vases and plant pots. To create visually pleasing layers, place plant pots in the back row on a pile of books. If you don’t have enough flowers, a good tip is to add artificial ones for a fuller look.
